Roasted Potatoes with Eggs

Roasted Potatoes with Scrambled Eggs

Serves: 2 Prep: 10 mins Cook: 25 mins

Ingredients:

Roasted Potatoes:
  • 1 cup baby potatoes, halved
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • Salt & pepper to taste
Scrambled Eggs:
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • 2 tbsp milk (optional)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
Toast:
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter (optional)
Garden Salad:
  • 1 cup mixed lettuce
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes
  • ¼ cup pomegranate seeds
  • 2 tbsp salad dressing

Instructions:

  1. Roast Potatoes: Preheat oven to 400°F. Toss potatoes with oil and spices. Roast for 20–25 mins, flipping halfway.
  2. Prepare Salad: Combine lettuce, tomatoes, and pomegranate seeds. Add dressing just before serving.
  3. Scramble Eggs: Whisk eggs with milk, salt and pepper. Cook in butter over medium heat for 2–3 mins.
  4. Toast Bread: Toast until golden. Butter if desired.
  5. Assemble & Serve: Plate eggs, potatoes, toast and salad together.

Spinach Pancakes

Spinach Pancakes with Side of Eggs

Serves: 2 Prep: 10 mins Cook: 15 mins

Ingredients:

Spinach Pancakes:
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1 tbsp melted butter/oil
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • Oil/butter for cooking
Side Eggs:
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter/oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
Optional Toppings:
  • Greek yogurt/sour cream
  • Avocado
  • Hot sauce/lemon wedges

Instructions:

  1. Make Batter: Blend spinach, milk and egg. Mix dry ingredients separately. Combine both, then add melted butter.
  2. Cook Pancakes: Heat pan, pour ¼ cup batter per pancake. Cook 2-3 mins per side until golden.
  3. Cook Eggs: In same pan, cook eggs sunny-side up or scrambled for 2-3 mins. Season.
  4. Serve: Stack pancakes with eggs. Add toppings if desired.
Tip: For extra fluffy pancakes, let the batter rest for 5 minutes before cooking!

Avocado & Chickpea Quinoa Bowl

VEGAN

Avocado & Chickpea Quinoa Bowl with Roasted Broccoli

Serves: 2 Prep: 10 mins Cook: 20 mins 🌱 High Fiber

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup quinoa (uncooked)
  • 1 cup water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1 small broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 ripe avocado, diced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: lemon juice, red pepper flakes, tahini

Instructions:

  1. Cook quinoa: Rinse quinoa, combine with water/broth. Bring to boil then simmer covered for 15 mins. Fluff with fork.
  2. Roast broccoli: Toss with oil, garlic powder, S&P. Roast at 400°F for 15-20 mins until crispy-tender.
  3. Season chickpeas: Sauté drained chickpeas with cumin, S&P for 3-5 mins until slightly crispy.
  4. Assemble bowl: Layer quinoa, broccoli, chickpeas and avocado. Add optional toppings.
Nutrition Tip: This bowl provides complete protein (quinoa + chickpeas), healthy fats (avocado), and fiber (broccoli).

Smoked Salmon Bagel

🥯 Homemade

From-Scratch Bagel with Smoked Salmon

Serves: 6 bagels Prep: 1.5 hrs Cook: 25 mins

Ingredients:

Bagel Ingredients:
  • 3½ cups (420g) bread flour
  • 1½ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp honey or barley malt syrup
  • 1¼ cups warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 egg white (for egg wash)
  • 2 tbsp everything bagel seasoning
Topping Ingredients:
  • 6 oz smoked salmon
  • ½ cup cream cheese
  • ¼ red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • 1 tbsp fresh dill
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Instructions:

  1. Make dough: Mix yeast with warm water and honey. Let sit 5 mins until foamy. Combine flour and salt in a large bowl. Add yeast mixture and knead for 10 mins until smooth and elastic.
  2. First rise: Place dough in greased bowl, cover with towel, and let rise 1 hour until doubled.
  3. Shape bagels: Punch down dough and divide into 6 equal pieces. Roll each into a ball, then poke a hole through center and stretch to form a ring.
  4. Second rise: Place shaped bagels on parchment-lined baking sheet, cover, and rest for 30 mins.
  5. Boil: Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Bring large pot of water to boil. Boil bagels 1 minute per side, 2 minutes total.
  6. Bake: Transfer to baking sheet, brush with egg wash, sprinkle seasoning. Bake 20-25 mins until golden brown.
  7. Cool: Let cool on wire rack for at least 15 mins before slicing.

Assembly:

  1. Slice cooled bagels and toast lightly.
  2. Spread cream cheese on both halves.
  3. Layer with smoked salmon, red onion, capers and dill.
  4. Squeeze lemon juice over top and serve immediately.
Baker's Tip: For chewier bagels, add 1 tbsp baking soda to the boiling water! The alkaline water creates a pretzel-like crust. NB: If you don't want to make bagels from scratch you can just run down to the store and buy them.

Egg Muffins

🍳 Meal Prep

Fluffy Egg Muffins

Makes: 12 muffins Prep: 15 mins Cook: 20 mins Total: 35 mins

Ingredients:

Base Ingredients:
  • 8 large eggs
  • ¼ cup milk (any kind)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
Suggested Add-Ins (choose 2-3):
  • ½ cup diced bell peppers
  • ⅓ cup crumbled cooked bacon
  • ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • ¼ cup chopped spinach
  • 2 tbsp diced onions
  • ¼ cup diced ham
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh herbs (chives, parsley)

Instructions:

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 12-cup muffin tin or line with silicone liners.
  2. Whisk eggs: In large bowl, whisk eggs, milk, salt and pepper until fully combined and slightly frothy.
  3. Add mix-ins: Stir in your chosen ingredients (about ¼ cup total add-ins per muffin).
  4. Fill cups: Pour egg mixture into muffin cups, filling each ¾ full (about ¼ cup per muffin).
  5. Bake: 18-22 minutes until centers are set and edges are golden. A toothpick should come out clean.
  6. Cool: Let rest in pan 5 minutes before transferring to wire rack.
Meal Prep Tips:
  • Store: Keep in airtight container for 3-4 days in fridge
  • Freeze: Wrap individually and freeze for up to 3 months
  • Reheat: Microwave 30-45 seconds or oven at 300°F for 10 mins
Try These Variations:
  • Mediterranean: Feta, sun-dried tomatoes, spinach
  • Southwest: Black beans, corn, jalapeños, pepper jack
  • Keto: Heavy cream instead of milk, add sausage

Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

VEGAN

Stuffed Sweet Potatoes with Chickpeas & Spinach

Serves: 4 Prep: 15 mins Cook: 45 mins

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium sweet potatoes (about 2 lbs)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 can (15oz) chickpeas, drained
  • 3 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • ½ red onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  • Salt & pepper to taste
For Serving:
  • ¼ cup tahini
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 tbsp toasted pine nuts (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Roast potatoes: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Scrub potatoes, pierce with fork, rub with oil, and bake directly on rack for 40-50 mins until tender.
  2. Make filling: While potatoes bake, heat oil in pan. Sauté onion 3 mins, add garlic and spices, cook 1 min. Add chickpeas and spinach, cook until spinach wilts (2-3 mins). Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Prepare sauce: Whisk tahini with lemon juice and 2 tbsp water until smooth.
  4. Assemble: Slice potatoes open, fluff flesh with fork. Stuff with chickpea mixture, drizzle with tahini sauce, and garnish with parsley and pine nuts.
Nutrition Per Serving: 380 kcal | 12g protein | 18g fiber | Excellent source of Vitamin A (400% DV)
Variations:
  • Spicy: Add ½ tsp chili flakes or harissa paste
  • Creamy: Swap tahini for coconut yogurt
  • Extra protein: Add ¼ cup crumbled feta (non-vegan)

Chicken Avocado Salad

🥑 High Protein

Creamy Chicken Avocado Salad

Serves: 4 Prep: 15 mins Cook: 12 mins Total: 27 mins

Ingredients:

Salad Ingredients:
  • 2 boneless chicken breasts (about 1 lb)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • ½ English cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ¼ red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups mixed greens
Creamy Lime Dressing:
  • ⅓ cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp lime zest
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook chicken: Season chicken with garlic powder, paprika, salt and pepper. Heat oil in pan over medium-high. Cook chicken 6 mins per side until 165°F internal temp. Let rest 5 mins, then dice.
  2. Make dressing: Whisk yogurt, lime juice, honey, zest, cumin, salt and pepper until smooth.
  3. Prep veggies: Dice avocado (squeeze lime juice over to prevent browning), cucumber, and halve tomatoes.
  4. Assemble: In large bowl, combine greens, chicken, avocado, cucumber, tomatoes and onion. Drizzle with dressing and toss gently.
Serving Suggestions:
  • In lettuce cups for low-carb option
  • As a sandwich filling in crusty bread
  • With tortilla chips for a hearty dip
  • Over quinoa for extra protein
Storage Tip: Store undressed salad in airtight container for up to 2 days. Add avocado and dressing just before serving.

English Breakfast

🇬🇧 No Pork

Full English Breakfast (Pork-Free)

Serves: 2 Prep: 10 mins Cook: 20 mins

Ingredients:

  • Eggs: 4 large eggs
  • Bread: 4 slices sourdough or brown bread
  • Meat Substitute: 4 vegetarian sausages
  • Mushrooms: 1 cup button mushrooms, halved
  • Tomatoes: 2 medium tomatoes, halved
  • Beans: 1 cup baked beans in tomato sauce
  • Seasonings: Salt, pepper, paprika, thyme
  • Fats: 2 tbsp butter + 1 tbsp olive oil

Cooking Method:

  1. Sausages: Cook vegetarian sausages in skillet with 1 tbsp oil, turning occasionally for 8-10 mins until browned.
  2. Mushrooms: In same pan, sauté mushrooms with thyme and pinch of salt until golden (4-5 mins). Remove and keep warm.
  3. Tomatoes: Grill tomato halves cut-side down in same pan for 3-4 mins until charred.
  4. Beans: Heat beans in small saucepan over low heat (add splash of water if too thick).
  5. Eggs: In clean pan, melt butter over medium-low. Crack eggs and cook sunny-side up for 3-4 mins until whites set but yolks are runny.
  6. Toast: Butter and toast bread until golden.

Assembly:

Divide all components between two warm plates. Serve immediately with tea or coffee.

Substitution Ideas:
  • Sausages: Turkey sausages, veggie patties, or halloumi slices
  • Beans: Swap for lentil stew or chickpeas in tomato sauce
  • Hash Browns: Try roasted potato wedges or sweet potato hash
Timing Tip: Start with longest-cooking items first (sausages → mushrooms → tomatoes → eggs/toast)
