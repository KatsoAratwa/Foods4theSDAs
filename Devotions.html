Bible Devotions for Healthy Living

Scriptural wisdom for body, mind and spirit

God's Original Diet

Discover the biblical foundation for plant-based nutrition and its health benefits.

KJV - Genesis 1:29

"And God said, Behold, I have given you every herb bearing seed, which is upon the face of all the earth, and every tree, in the which is the fruit of a tree yielding seed; to you it shall be for meat."

NIV - Genesis 1:29

"Then God said, 'I give you every seed-bearing plant on the face of the whole earth and every tree that has fruit with seed in it. They will be yours for food.'"

The Living Water

Physical and spiritual lessons from Christ's teaching about water.

KJV - John 4:14

"But whosoever drinketh of the water that I shall give him shall never thirst; but the water that I shall give him shall be in him a well of water springing up into everlasting life."

NIV - John 4:14

"but whoever drinks the water I give them will never thirst. Indeed, the water I give them will become in them a spring of water welling up to eternal life."

Sabbath Rest

God's design for weekly physical and spiritual renewal.

KJV - Exodus 20:8-10

"Remember the sabbath day, to keep it holy. Six days shalt thou labour, and do all thy work: But the seventh day is the sabbath of the LORD thy God: in it thou shalt not do any work..."

NIV - Exodus 20:8-10

"Remember the Sabbath day by keeping it holy. Six days you shall labor and do all your work, but the seventh day is a sabbath to the LORD your God. On it you shall not do any work..."

Temple of the Holy Spirit

Caring for our bodies as an act of worship.

KJV - 1 Corinthians 6:19-20

"What? know ye not that your body is the temple of the Holy Ghost which is in you, which ye have of God, and ye are not your own? For ye are bought with a price: therefore glorify God in your body, and in your spirit, which are God's."

NIV - 1 Corinthians 6:19-20

"Do you not know that your bodies are temples of the Holy Spirit, who is in you, whom you have received from God? You are not your own; you were bought at a price. Therefore honor God with your bodies."

The Virtue of Temperance

Biblical principles of moderation and self-control.

KJV - Proverbs 25:16

"Hast thou found honey? eat so much as is sufficient for thee, lest thou be filled therewith, and vomit it."

NIV - Proverbs 25:16

"If you find honey, eat just enough—too much of it, and you will vomit."

God's Gift of Sleep

Divine wisdom about rest and nighttime rejuvenation.

KJV - Psalm 127:2

"It is vain for you to rise up early, to sit up late, to eat the bread of sorrows: for so he giveth his beloved sleep."

NIV - Psalm 127:2

"In vain you rise early and stay up late, toiling for food to eat—for he grants sleep to those he loves."

Peace That Passes Understanding

Managing stress through faith.

KJV - Philippians 4:6-7

"Be careful for nothing; but in every thing by prayer and supplication with thanksgiving let your requests be made known unto God. And the peace of God, which passeth all understanding, shall keep your hearts and minds through Christ Jesus."

NIV - Philippians 4:6-7

"Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God. And the peace of God, which transcends all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds in Christ Jesus."

Biblical Fasting

Spiritual and physical benefits of periodic fasting.

KJV - Matthew 6:16-18

"Moreover when ye fast, be not, as the hypocrites, of a sad countenance: for they disfigure their faces, that they may appear unto men to fast. Verily I say unto you, They have their reward."

NIV - Matthew 6:16-18

"When you fast, do not look somber as the hypocrites do, for they disfigure their faces to show others they are fasting. Truly I tell you, they have received their reward in full."

Fellowship and Health

How Christian community supports wellness.

KJV - Hebrews 10:24-25

"And let us consider one another to provoke unto love and to good works: Not forsaking the assembling of ourselves together, as the manner of some is; but exhorting one another: and so much the more, as ye see the day approaching."

NIV - Hebrews 10:24-25

"And let us consider how we may spur one another on toward love and good deeds, not giving up meeting together, as some are in the habit of doing, but encouraging one another—and all the more as you see the Day approaching."

The Healing Power of Gratitude

Thankfulness as a path to better health.

KJV - 1 Thessalonians 5:18

"In every thing give thanks: for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus concerning you."

NIV - 1 Thessalonians 5:18

"give thanks in all circumstances; for this is God's will for you in Christ Jesus."

Nature Therapy

God's creation as a healing environment.

KJV - Psalm 23:2-3

"He maketh me to lie down in green pastures: he leadeth me beside the still waters. He restoreth my soul: he leadeth me in the paths of righteousness for his name's sake."

NIV - Psalm 23:2-3

"He makes me lie down in green pastures, he leads me beside quiet waters, he refreshes my soul. He guides me along the right paths for his name's sake."

Healthy Aging

Biblical examples of vitality in later years.

KJV - Isaiah 46:4

"And even to your old age I am he; and even to hoar hairs will I carry you: I have made, and I will bear; even I will carry, and will deliver you."

NIV - Isaiah 46:4

"Even to your old age and gray hairs I am he, I am he who will sustain you. I have made you and I will carry you; I will sustain you and I will rescue you."
