Lentil Walnut Loaf Vegan

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 60 mins Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry green lentils
  • 1 cup walnuts, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp thyme

Instructions

  1. Cook lentils until tender (about 25 mins), drain
  2. Sauté onion and garlic until soft
  3. Combine all ingredients in bowl, mix well
  4. Press into greased loaf pan
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 mins
Alternative: Replace walnuts with sunflower seeds for nut-free version

Quinoa Stuffed Peppers Vegan

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 30 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers
  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1 avocado (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Cut tops off peppers and remove seeds
  2. Mix quinoa, beans, corn, cumin and salsa
  3. Stuff peppers with mixture
  4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 mins
  5. Top with avocado slices before serving

Vegetable Lasagna

Prep: 30 mins Cook: 45 mins Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese (or tofu ricotta)
  • 2 cups spinach, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles according to package
  2. Layer noodles, ricotta, vegetables and sauce
  3. Repeat layers, ending with sauce
  4. Top with mozzarella if using
  5. Bake covered at 375°F (190°C) for 40 mins
  6. Uncover and bake 5 more mins
Vegan Option: Use tofu ricotta and vegan cheese

Chickpea Salad Sandwich Vegan

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 0 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained
  • 1/4 cup vegan mayo
  • 1 stalk celery, diced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 8 slices whole grain bread
  • Lettuce and tomato for serving

Instructions

  1. Mash chickpeas with fork or potato masher
  2. Mix with all other ingredients except bread
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  4. Serve on bread with lettuce and tomato

Sweet Potato Burgers Vegan

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 25 mins Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed sweet potato
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 6 whole wheat buns

Instructions

  1. Mash beans with sweet potato
  2. Add spices and breadcrumbs, mix well
  3. Form into 6 patties
  4. Chill for 30 mins (optional but helps firm up)
  5. Cook in oiled pan 5-6 mins per side
  6. Serve on buns with favorite toppings

Tofu Vegetable Stir Fry Vegan

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 15 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 block firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice

Instructions

  1. Stir-fry tofu in oil until golden, set aside
  2. Stir-fry vegetables 5-6 mins until crisp-tender
  3. Add sauce ingredients and tofu back to pan
  4. Cook 2 mins until sauce thickens slightly
  5. Serve over brown rice
Alternative: Replace tofu with chicken for non-vegan version

Hearty Vegetable Soup

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 30 mins Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups chopped cabbage
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion, carrots and celery 5 mins
  2. Add garlic, cook 1 min more
  3. Add remaining ingredients
  4. Simmer 25-30 mins until vegetables are tender
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste
Variation: Add 1 cup cooked lentils for extra protein

Quinoa Stuffed Avocados Vegan

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 15 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados, halved
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup black beans
  • 1/4 cup corn
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Scoop out some avocado to make room for filling
  2. Mix quinoa, beans, corn, lime juice and spices
  3. Spoon mixture into avocado halves
  4. Garnish with cilantro
  5. Serve immediately

Vegetable Coconut Curry Vegan

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 25 mins Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1 sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 cup chickpeas
  • 2 cups cooked brown rice

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in oil 3 mins
  2. Add curry powder, cook 1 min
  3. Add coconut milk and vegetables
  4. Simmer 20 mins until vegetables are tender
  5. Serve over brown rice

Spinach Mushroom Quesadillas

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 15 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 whole wheat tortillas
  • 2 cups spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (or vegan cheese)
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup salsa for serving

Instructions

  1. Sauté mushrooms and spinach until wilted
  2. Spread cheese on half of each tortilla
  3. Top with vegetable mixture
  4. Fold tortillas in half
  5. Cook in dry skillet 2-3 mins per side until golden
  6. Serve with salsa

Vegetable Paella Vegan

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 30 mins Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cups short grain rice
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 1 cup artichoke hearts
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion and bell pepper in oil 5 mins
  2. Add rice and paprika, stir 1 min
  3. Add broth and bring to boil
  4. Reduce heat, add vegetables
  5. Simmer 20 mins until rice is tender
  6. Let stand 5 mins before serving with lemon

Stuffed Zucchini Boats

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 25 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 large zucchinis
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan (or nutritional yeast)
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs

Instructions

  1. Cut zucchinis in half lengthwise, scoop out centers
  2. Mix quinoa, marinara, and seasonings
  3. Fill zucchini halves with mixture
  4. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and cheese
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20-25 mins

Mediterranean Wrap Vegan

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 0 mins Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 whole wheat tortillas
  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup kalamata olives
  • 1 cup baby spinach

Instructions

  1. Spread hummus on tortillas
  2. Layer with remaining ingredients
  3. Roll up tightly
  4. Cut in half and serve
Variation: Add feta cheese for non-vegan version
