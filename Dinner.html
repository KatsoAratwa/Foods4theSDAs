Sweet Potato Enchiladas Vegan

Prep: 25 mins Cook: 35 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, roasted
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp chili powder
  • 6 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce

Instructions

  1. Mash sweet potatoes and mix with beans and spices
  2. Fill tortillas with mixture, roll and place in baking dish
  3. Top with enchilada sauce, bake at 375°F for 30 mins

Baked Tofu Stir-Fry Vegan

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 25 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 block extra-firm tofu, cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic, ginger (minced)

Instructions

  1. Bake tofu at 400°F for 20 minutes until crisp
  2. Stir-fry veggies in oil and garlic/ginger
  3. Add tofu and soy sauce, toss and serve

Chickpea Coconut Curry Vegan

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 25 mins Serves: 3

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp salt, 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion in olive oil
  2. Add chickpeas, curry powder and salt
  3. Pour in coconut milk, simmer 20 mins

Mushroom Lentil Shepherd’s Pie

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 45 mins Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 cup lentils
  • 1 cup mushrooms, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1 tsp thyme, 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion and mushrooms
  2. Add lentils and seasonings, cook until soft
  3. Spread lentil mix in baking dish, top with mashed potatoes
  4. Bake at 375°F for 30 mins

Spinach & Ricotta Shells

Prep: 25 mins Cook: 30 mins Serves: 5

Ingredients

  • 12 jumbo pasta shells
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup spinach, cooked and chopped
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup mozzarella (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta shells until al dente
  2. Mix ricotta and spinach
  3. Stuff shells and place in baking dish with sauce
  4. Top with cheese and bake at 375°F for 25 mins

Brown Rice Stir Fry Vegan

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 20 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1 cup chopped broccoli
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Sauté vegetables in sesame oil

Creamy Mushroom Stroganoff

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 30 mins Serves: 3

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1 cup plant-based cream or cashew cream
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Pasta or rice to serve

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions and mushrooms
  2. Add cream and paprika, simmer for 10 mins
  3. Serve over pasta or rice

Grilled Veggie Skewers Vegan

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 15 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • Bell peppers, onions, zucchini, cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper
  • Bamboo skewers (soaked)

Instructions

  1. Chop veggies and skewer
  2. Brush with oil and season
  3. Grill or bake at 400°F for 12–15 mins

Baked Ziti (Cashew Cheese) Vegan

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 30 mins Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 12 oz ziti pasta
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup cashew cheese (blended cashews + lemon + garlic)

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta and drain
  2. Layer pasta, sauce, and cheese in baking dish
  3. Bake at 375°F for 30 mins

Lentil Spaghetti Bolognese Vegan

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 35 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup lentils, cooked
  • 1/2 cup onion, diced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • Spaghetti pasta

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion, add lentils and tomato sauce
  2. Simmer for 20 mins
  3. Serve over cooked spaghetti

Chickpea Veggie Patties Vegan

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 10 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, mashed
  • 1 grated carrot, 1/4 cup oats
